About Kudos Cubs

At Kudos Cubs, we believe every child has a unique talent that deserves to shine. Founded by a team of parents and child development experts, we're dedicated to creating a safe, fun, and rewarding platform for children to express their creativity and talents.

  • Empowerment Through Recognition
  • Community and Support
  • Resources for Growth

Experience the joy of seeing your child recognized for their creativity and skills as they earn accolades and build self-esteem through positive community feedback and awards.

Join a nurturing community of like-minded parents and experts, all dedicated to encouraging the natural talents of each child in a supportive and collaborative environment.

Gain access to valuable resources and expert tips designed to help you guide your child's development in the arts, enhance their presentation skills, and provide them with opportunities for growth and learning.

Frequently Asked Questions

We welcome your questions via email. In the meantime, here's a selection of common questions.

  • What kind of videos can my child showcase on KudosCubs?

    KudosCubs welcomes all kinds of talents! From singing, dancing, and playing musical instruments, to painting, storytelling, or even making people laugh. If your child has a unique talent or a charming moment to share, we're here to celebrate it.

  • How do I upload a video and enter my child into a contest?

    Uploading a video is easy! Click on the "Submit Video" button and you'll find the 'Upload' option. Just select the contest you'd like to enter, upload the video, and you’re set! Our user-friendly interface makes participation a breeze.

  • Is there a fee to participate in the contests?

    No, it's absolutely free to enter our contests. We believe every child deserves a stage, and we wouldn't want entry fees to stand in the way of your child's opportunity to shine.

  • How are the winners chosen?

    Winners are chosen through a combination of community votes and a panel of impartial judges. This ensures a fair and democratic selection process, where talent truly gets to shine.

  • What are the prizes for winning a contest?

    Prizes can range from scholarship funds to support your child's education, vouchers for art supplies or music lessons, to features on our platform and social media channels. Each contest may have different prizes, so be sure to check the details for the contest you're entering!

  • How do you ensure the safety and privacy of children on the platform?

    Child safety is our top priority. We comply with all COPPA regulations and have strict privacy policies in place. Videos are screened for safety, and we never share personal information without explicit consent.

  • Can family and friends vote for my child's video?

    Absolutely! We encourage you to share your child's talent with your network. Family, friends, and the KudosCubs community can all cast their votes for their favorite young stars.

  • How often can my child participate in contests?

    There's no limit to how often your child can participate. We host a variety of contests throughout the year, so there are always new opportunities to join in.

  • What if my child doesn't win? Is there any recognition for participating?

    Every child that participates is a star in our eyes. We celebrate all entries by showcasing them on our platform, and often have recognition for runners-up and special categories. The experience itself is rewarding, offering exposure and the chance to be part of a loving community.
